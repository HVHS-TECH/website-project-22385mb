Jazz Band

With Chris Buckland

The HVHS Jazz Band is an awesome oppurtuntiy for students interested in playing jazz or being part of a large ensemble. From Stevie Wonder to Herbie Hancock to Snarky Puppy and many more the Jazz Band explores the foundations of performing Jazz music.

The band is always on the lookout for new members so don't hesitate to pop in and see about joining up.

Jazz Band meets on Wednesdays at 1:30pm in M1
