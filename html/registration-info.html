Students taking music instrumental lessons through school will be expected to take an active part a co-curricular music group at HVHS. Through these activities, students use the skills learned in lessons and improve at a faster rate.

Tuiton is free and open year round.

Beginners are welcome on all instruments!

As part of taking lessons students are expected to:

  • Know when their lesson is and be on time each week.
  • Not miss more than 3 lessons without justification or else forfeit their place.
  • Practice outside of their lessons.
  • Try their best and have fun!

Lesson Information

  • Tuition will be in pairs or small groups.
  • Lessons are 30 minutes each week during the normal school day.
  • To minimise disruption, times are rotated so students do not miss the same subject each week.
