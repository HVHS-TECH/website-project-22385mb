Music is a broad and exiting subject covering all elements and standards of music with a focus on musical literacy. Music Performance is also offered in Years 11 & 12 (senior school) and focuses on group work and creative thinking skills.

It's avaliable right from the start of Year 9 to the end of Year 13!

9MUSC

Year 9 Music

Students first steps into the wonderful world of music at HVHS

Year 9 Music is the perfect place to try out the subject and see if you like it.

9MUSC is offered as both a 2 Term subject and a 1 Term subject so there are plenty of opportunities to give it a go.

This course covers a wide variety of musical aspects including:

  • guitar skills
  • keyboard skills
  • solo performance - own instrument
  • music history
  • listening skills
  • theory
  • music technology
  • composing
