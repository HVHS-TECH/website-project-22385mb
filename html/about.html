The Music Department

Walking past the Music Block at Hutt Valley High School, it's hard not to stop and listen to the variety of sounds and music coming from there. From students practicing their craft, playing in groups or performing for their classmates and friends there is always something going on.

And the best part?

Everyone is welcome to join, even if you've never tried music before!
